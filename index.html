Pokemon Chart

Pokemon are a wonderful thing. They're like pets but with supernatural abilities! Out camping and don't know how to make a fire? Don't worry, Charmander can help you out. Forgot you waterwings when you jumped into that pool? Squirtle to the rescue. Really is a great thing to be a pokemon trainer, but not just everyone can be a pokemon trainer. Here's a list of what makes a solid trainer.

  1. Be compassionate
  2. Be responsible
    • Last thing you want is a hungry crabby pokemon
  3. Let them out of there pokeball often
  4. Try not to show favoritism
